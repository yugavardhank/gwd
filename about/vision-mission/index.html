Vision, Mission & Core Values

“Empowering healthcare professionals to transform lives through knowledge, compassion, and innovation.”

To be the world’s most trusted and advanced platform for healthcare education and workforce development, positively impacting a billion lives.

To equip healthcare professionals across the globe with the education, certifications, and guidance they need to serve with excellence, empathy, and ethics.

  • Compassion: Putting people first, always.
  • Innovation: Advancing with technology and ideas.
  • Integrity: Upholding ethics in all our efforts.
  • Excellence: Striving to lead, not follow.

Apollo Hospitals is founded in Chennai by Dr. Prathap C. Reddy, introducing corporate healthcare to India and setting new benchmarks in quality and patient care.

First Apollo hospital outside Chennai opens in Hyderabad, marking the start of a pan-India expansion and regional healthcare transformation.

Apollo launches India’s first hospital-based telemedicine center, pioneering remote healthcare delivery and digital consultations.

Apollo Nursing Colleges are established, setting new standards in nursing education and producing globally competent healthcare professionals.

Apollo Hospitals becomes the first in India to be accredited by JCI (Joint Commission International), reinforcing its commitment to world-class quality and safety.

Launch of Apollo Munich Health Insurance and Apollo Health City, Hyderabad, India’s first health city integrating hospitals, education, and research.

Apollo expands global partnerships, enabling international placements, certifications, and collaborative research with leading institutions worldwide.

Apollo launches Apollo Knowledge, Apollo MedSkills, and Apollo Medvarsity, driving digital healthcare education and upskilling for global healthcare professionals.

Apollo Hospitals crosses 50 hospitals, 10,000 beds, and 100 telemedicine centers, serving millions across India, Africa, and Southeast Asia.

Apollo launches the Global Workforce Development initiative, focusing on international healthcare placements, training, and global mobility for professionals.

Apollo’s digital health ecosystem reaches 20 million users, integrating AI, telehealth, and e-learning for seamless global healthcare access and education.

Over 75,000 professionals trained, 150+ countries served, and Apollo stands as a global leader in healthcare education, innovation, and workforce development.

Values & Culture

At Apollo Global Workforce Development, our values and culture are the foundation of our mission to empower healthcare professionals worldwide. We foster an environment of integrity, innovation, and inclusivity—enabling individuals to thrive and make a global impact.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and technologies to advance healthcare education and workforce solutions, ensuring our learners are future-ready.

Compassion

We put people first—supporting learners, partners, and communities with empathy and respect across borders and cultures.

Collaboration

We build strong partnerships with global institutions, educators, and employers to create opportunities and share knowledge worldwide.

Excellence

We set high standards in training, certification, and placement—driving quality and continuous improvement in everything we do.

Inclusivity

We celebrate diversity and provide equal opportunities for healthcare professionals from all backgrounds to succeed globally.

Integrity

We act with honesty, transparency, and ethical responsibility—earning the trust of our learners and partners worldwide.

Empowerment

We equip individuals with the skills, confidence, and guidance to achieve their career aspirations and make a difference in global healthcare.

Empowering healthcare professionals to achieve their global career aspirations through comprehensive training, certification, and placement support.

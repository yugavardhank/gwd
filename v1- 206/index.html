Who are we?

Global Workforce Development (GWD) is an initiative by the Apollo Hospitals Group, committed to preparing healthcare professionals for global careers. We provide advanced training, international certifications, and comprehensive career guidance for nurses, doctors, and allied health professionals.

  • Global career pathways for healthcare professionals
  • Accredited training and mentorship
  • End-to-end migration and placement support
  • Backed by Apollo Hospitals’ legacy of clinical excellence
  • 100,000+ professionals trained, 70+ hospitals worldwide

Promoters & Leadership Team

The Apollo Hospitals Group is shaped by visionary promoters and a forward-thinking leadership team. Their dedication to innovation, quality, and global healthcare advancement has set new standards and inspired professionals across the world.

“True leadership inspires generations to heal, innovate, and serve with compassion.”

Our Promoters

Dr. Prathap C Reddy

Founder & Chairman, Apollo Hospitals Group

Dr. Prathap C Reddy is a pioneering cardiologist and visionary entrepreneur who transformed healthcare in India and beyond. As the Founder and Chairman of Apollo Hospitals Group, he established India’s first corporate hospital in 1983, setting new benchmarks in clinical excellence, patient-centric care, and medical innovation. Under his leadership, Apollo has become Asia’s largest integrated healthcare provider, with a global reputation for quality and compassion.

Dr. Reddy’s relentless commitment to affordable, accessible healthcare has empowered millions and inspired generations of healthcare professionals. He is a recipient of numerous national and international honors, including the Padma Vibhushan, for his outstanding contributions to medicine and society.

Dr. Preetha Reddy

Executive Vice Chairperson, Apollo Hospitals Group

A global healthcare leader driving strategic growth, clinical excellence, and international collaborations for Apollo.

Dr. Suneeta Reddy

Managing Director, Apollo Hospitals Group

Renowned for her expertise in finance, strategy, and healthcare management, steering Apollo’s expansion and innovation.

Ms. Shobana Kamineni

Executive Vice Chairperson, Apollo Hospitals Group

Championing digital health, sustainability, and global partnerships to advance Apollo’s mission worldwide.

Dr. Sangita Reddy

Joint Managing Director, Apollo Hospitals Group

A dynamic leader in healthcare delivery, technology, and advocacy for accessible, quality healthcare for all.

Leadership Team

Mr. Sivaramakrishnan Venkateswaran

Chief Executive Officer
Apollo Knowledge

With over 34 years of experience, including 16+ years in the education services industry, Sivaramakrishnan is a seasoned leader who drives innovation, academic excellence, and transformative learning initiatives.

Ms. Shobana Kamineni

Executive Vice Chairperson

Ms. Shobana Kamineni is a visionary leader at Apollo Hospitals Group, known for her pioneering work in digital health, sustainability, and global partnerships. She has spearheaded Apollo’s digital transformation, including the launch of innovative telemedicine and health-tech platforms. Ms. Kamineni is also a strong proponent of sustainable healthcare practices and has represented Apollo at international business and policy forums, advocating for accessible, technology-enabled healthcare.

Dr. Sangita Reddy

Joint Managing Director

Dr. Sangita Reddy is a dynamic leader in healthcare delivery, technology adoption, and public health advocacy. As Joint Managing Director, she has led Apollo’s initiatives in digital health, telemedicine, and community outreach, expanding access to quality healthcare across India and beyond. Dr. Reddy is a prominent voice in healthcare policy, innovation, and women’s empowerment, and has received numerous accolades for her contributions to the industry.

Dr. Ravi Kumar

Director, Global Workforce Development

Dr. Ravi Kumar leads Apollo’s Global Workforce Development initiatives, focusing on international healthcare education, training, and talent mobility. With extensive experience in medical education and global partnerships, he has developed programs that empower healthcare professionals to achieve international standards and career growth. Dr. Kumar is dedicated to bridging skill gaps and fostering cross-border collaborations in healthcare.

Ms. Anita Sharma

Director, Nursing Education & Training

Ms. Anita Sharma oversees Apollo’s nursing education and training programs, ensuring the highest standards of clinical competence and patient care. With decades of experience in nursing leadership, she has designed curricula and certification pathways that prepare nurses for global opportunities. Ms. Sharma is passionate about professional development, mentorship, and advancing the role of nurses in modern healthcare.

Mr. Arvind Menon

Director, International Recruitment

Mr. Arvind Menon leads Apollo’s international recruitment and placement services, connecting skilled healthcare professionals with global employers. He has built robust networks with hospitals, regulatory bodies, and training partners worldwide. Mr. Menon’s expertise ensures seamless talent mobility, compliance with international standards, and successful career transitions for Apollo-trained professionals.

Empowering healthcare professionals to achieve their global career aspirations through comprehensive training, certification, and placement support.
